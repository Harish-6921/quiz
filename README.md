# quiz
AI Sports Quiz — an interactive Bootstrap quiz app with 180 built-in questions across cricket, football, tennis, basketball, badminton &amp; hockey, with timer, scoring, and dark/light mode.

# SECOUND STEP OF QUIZ
 Choose your sport
Click the Select Sport dropdown and pick one:
Cricket, Football, Tennis, Basketball, Badminton, or Hockey.
Click the Difficulty Level dropdown and pick:
Easy, Medium, or Hard.
![image alt](https://github.com/Harish-6921/quiz/blob/b9037203f83ba03c1524d5cb098197f2994bf07c/quizss2.png)
# THIRD STEP OF QUIZ
The quiz screen loads instantly (no waiting) with:
Question 1 / 10 shown at the top
A 20-second countdown timer on the right
A progress bar that fills as you go
![image alt](https://github.com/Harish-6921/quiz/blob/a0b3b1d82f098458ac3d01acc89e067d830af8aa/quizss3.png)
# FOURTH STEP OF QUIZ
Click one of the 4 options.
✅ The correct answer turns green
❌ If you picked wrong, your choice turns red
If the 20-second timer runs out before you click anything, it auto-locks the question as unanswered and still reveals the correct answer.
This advances you to the next question and restarts the 20-second timer. Repeat steps 5–6 until you've done all 10.
![image alt](https://github.com/Harish-6921/quiz/blob/2f8121d952ffe3ceb6ffa0f178ea4e6f280ee075/quizss4.png)
# FINAL STEP OF QUIZ 
After question 10, you'll land on the "Quiz Complete!" screen showing:
Your score (e.g., 5 / 10)
A short message based on performance (perfect score, great job, keep practicing, etc.)
![image alt](https://github.com/Harish-6921/quiz/blob/299577f6c9eb76f85e39384a831c64b8a69f5e96/quizss5.png)
